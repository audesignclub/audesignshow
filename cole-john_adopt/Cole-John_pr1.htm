What

Pet Fiesta is an annual outdoor festival that brings local businesses, animal rescue groups and pet owners together to not only participate in fun events like the popular Dog House of Style Fashion Show but to raise money to help homeless dogs and rescue groups.On top of all that awesomeness, admission is free to enjoy the awesome list of fiesta events:

  • Tails on Trails Dog Walk
  • Pet Adoption
  • Dog House of Style Fashion Show
  • Kid's Activities
  • Live Entertaiments
How

Directed by GoodDogz.com, and with the help of over 120 exhibitors, Pet Fiesta has brought 10,000 plus dog lovers to the Reston Town Center every year. Alongside, Hope Advanced Veterinary Center and NovaDog magazine, GoodDogz.com has helped make Pet Fiesta a hot ticket in Reston. A great place to make new friends, enjoy great entertainment and most importantly, adopt a dog!

Why

In addition to events and vendors, fiesta attendees can also adopt a dog on site, the day of. Main contributor and non-profit organization, GoodDogz.com helps through the adoption process. Dedicated to educating potential dog owners on dog selection, care and supporting the efforts of rescue groups, GoodDogz.com has been a Pet Fiesta sponsor since its incepton.

"Whether you have a perro or a gato join other pet owners, local businesses, and animal rescue groups for a day of activities, demonstrations and animals galore. Meet wonderful animals looking for their forever homes..."

-Yelp.com

Where

Pet Fiesta is held annualy at the Reston Town Center in Reston Virginia. This year's fiesta will be on Saturday, May 7th 2017. The Reston Town Center is roughly a 30 minute drive from Washington, D.C. and is not metro accessible, but don't let that stop you! You can split an Uber with friends or even rent a car in D.C. through Zipcar. The Fiesta awaits you!
